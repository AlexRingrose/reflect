Welcome to Reflect

This is a tool for MSU History Majors. Use it to review your work, evalute your historical skills, and prepare the required reflection statements for your required graduation portfolio. You can use Reflect to complete the cover sheet for your portfolio, to enter the titles and courses for your selected papers, and to assess your mastery of the program’s different Student Learning Outcomes. You can then Preview your portfolio document and download it as a PDF file to include with your graduation portfolio. Reflect saves your changes locally your computer or device, but nothing is uploaded anywhere else. If you clear your browser’s history and cookies you will lose your saved information. About Reflect
